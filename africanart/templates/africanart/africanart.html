By The Ghettoside

By: Amanda Badze

I release my hated. Given to destruction, Creations's servant.

New form is always natural, Allowed, Good, & Loved.

That is because I desire to bow deeply. Swim eternally.

Present, In the depths of appreciation.

Oh So let it be known, as it is in Africa,

That the value of The Continent's identity,

Refuses to be known, until her ghetto is Felt.
