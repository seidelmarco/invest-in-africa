Ndiri Pano

Amanda Badze

Visit the new exhibition: By The Ghettoside




I AM not the same as those who are

Going to see if they are fearless or

not.


Somewhere I am alive now, and the

darkness I am is dead.


Here, is the resurrection ability, -A

present, past future, occupied by

not.


To the house of service, I weep at,

knowledge of life.


After I have never completed to

weep, I will never begin to sing.
