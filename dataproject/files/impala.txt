FINAL CASH DIVIDEND – STERLING CURRENCY CONVERSION RATES
On 3 September 2020 the board of directors of Implats determined to pay a final cash dividend of 400 cents per
ordinary share payable on 28 September 2020. The ruling rand to pound sterling conversion rate on 24 September 2020
of R21.6151 to GBP1.00 will be used in calculating the sterling amount payable to shareholders registered in the
United Kingdom section of the share register. This converted amount equates to a pre-tax interim cash dividend
of 18,50558 pence per ordinary share to United Kingdom registered shareholders.
